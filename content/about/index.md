## Creative Central Asia 2026

The **Creative Central Asia 2026** forum brings together creative professionals from across Central Asia to explore how creativity connects the economy, technology, and identity — and how these connections can drive meaningful change.

**Date:** February 28, 2026
**Location:** Tashkent, Uzbekistan
**Website:** [ccaforum.uz](https://ccaforum.uz)

### Key Themes

- **Collaboration & Cross-disciplinarity** — Breaking silos between creative fields
- **Impact Investment & the Language of Values** — Funding the creative economy
- **Education, AI & Skills for the Future** — Preparing the next generation

## Creative Turkmenistan

Our delegation represents the breadth of Turkmenistan's creative industries — from animation studios and AI specialists to digital educators and full-cycle creative agencies.

We are united by a shared belief: that Central Asia's creative economy has enormous untapped potential, and that by connecting with peers across the region, we can build something greater than any of us could alone.

### Our Mission

To showcase Turkmenistan's creative talent on the international stage, forge partnerships with fellow Central Asian creators, and demonstrate how innovation, tradition, and technology can work together to build a thriving creative economy.
