I'm Kemal Babayev, a practicing director and cinematographer, 3D generalist, and AI content creator. I work on full-cycle commercial projects: from concept and production to final delivery packages including graphics and neural networks.

My portfolio includes a short film about Ashgabat and a 3D animated safety video for Turkmenistan Airlines.

## Studio22

Today I lead the 2D and 3D animation department at **Studio22**. Under my leadership works a team of talented artists, animators, and technical specialists.

Studio22 is a full-cycle creative agency from Turkmenistan. We don't just create content — we build a bridge between the traditional culture of Central Asia and the technologies of the future. Our focus today is the synergy of high-quality 2D/3D animation and the integration of artificial intelligence into creative processes.

At Studio22, we have already integrated neural networks into our animation and marketing production pipeline. This allows us to reduce costs without sacrificing quality, making Turkmen creative products competitive on the international level.

## Philosophy

We believe that the creative economy should be built on local meanings. Our flagship projects — "Paylash" and "Goshalar" — are a response to society's search for its own identity in a global world.

### Goshalar

The first animated series in Turkmenistan about secular life. We show the life of a modern Central Asian resident — from everyday concerns to secular culture — natively, with humor and kindness. "Goshalar" is a way to speak with youth in their language about important values while preserving our cultural code.

### Paylash

Through "Paylash," we are forming a new-generation educational environment. This product has enormous potential for scaling and creating merchandising, which is a direct investment in the future of the country's creative sector.
