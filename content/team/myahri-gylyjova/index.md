Hi! I'm Myahri. I'm a TechWomen Alumna and a Digital Skills Trainer from Turkmenistan. I specialize in STEM education, but my unique passion is a **Theater English Club** where we develop Soft Skills through drama.

I'm here to find partners who believe that the future of tech lies in human communication. Let's connect.

## What I Do

I bridge the gap between technology and human skills through innovative educational approaches. My work combines:

- **STEM Education** — Making science and technology accessible and engaging for young learners in Turkmenistan
- **Digital Skills Training** — Equipping the next generation with essential digital literacy and technical competencies
- **Theater English Club** — A unique program that develops soft skills — communication, teamwork, empathy, and leadership — through the power of drama and performance

## Vision

The future of technology is deeply human. While technical skills open doors, it's the ability to communicate, collaborate, and empathize that drives real innovation. Through my work, I aim to develop well-rounded individuals who can lead Central Asia's creative and technological transformation.
