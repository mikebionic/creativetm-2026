**Jam Creative** is a full-cycle creative agency based in Ashgabat, Turkmenistan. Our team positions itself as a professional association specializing in creating unique visual content, organizing large-scale events, and developing creative strategies.

## What We Do

### Event Management
- Organizing celebrations, masterclasses, and interactive games
- Professional team of animators — one of the most recognizable directions on social media
- Active participation in state and international exhibitions (including Kids Expo in Ashgabat)

### Innovation
We use modern entertainment formats — educational games, creative workshops for children — bringing fresh approaches to audience engagement in Turkmenistan.

### Recognition
Jam Creative has been mentioned in official Turkmenistan media (Turkmenportal, Orient) as an agency contributing to unlocking the creative potential of youth.

## Our Approach

Every project receives individual attention — from small private orders to large corporate events. We believe in a client-oriented approach where each project is tailored to its unique requirements and audience.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of what's possible in Turkmenistan's creative landscape, introducing international-quality production values while staying true to our local culture and community.
