About the Delegation

Turkmenistan's creative delegation brings together a diverse group of innovators attending the Creative Economy Forum 2026 in Uzbekistan.

Our team spans filmmaking, AI-generated art, digital education, and creative agency work — united by a shared vision of expanding Turkmenistan's presence in the global creative economy.

From Studio22's cinematic productions to cutting-edge AI art, from TechWomen digital skills training to Jam Creative Agency's brand work, we represent the breadth and depth of Central Asian creativity.
